Favorite Movie

My favorite movie is School of Rock.
It is about a a guitarist Dewey Finn. After he is kicked out of his rock band,
he becomes a substitute teacher at an uptight elementary school. He tries to turn his class into a rock band.
Check out the IMDB page.

Favorite Music

My favorite band is the Deftones.
Deftones is an American alternative metal band from Sacramento, CA. They formed in 1988 and are still releasing albums and touring.
They have put out such albums as Around the Fur, White Pony, Diamond Eyes, and Koi No Yokan.
